Wyatt Guthrie - Industrial Designer

I'm an industrial designer who transforms concepts into manufacturable, user-focused products through sketching, 3D modeling, and prototyping. Rooted in a human-centered design education from the Cleveland Institute of Art, I integrate AI-driven strategies, emerging technologies, and rapid prototyping to create impactful solutions across consumer, medical, and sustainable fields.

My Design Philosophy

I push design boundaries by embracing cutting-edge tools and balancing form, function, and feasibility. By leveraging AI, AR, and VR, I aim to deliver forward-thinking, user-centered products that spark meaningful change.
